The Need for a Permanent Building

Considering the multiplicity of activities of the CDASF, the need for a permanent building is felt. The proposed building will be used for the enhancement and development of Ahadhiyyah Education.

There is a need for a Hall, for meetings and lectures, the need to establish a Teacher Training Institute, the need to conduct specialized Student development programmes, the need for a Permanent Office to administer the affairs of the CDASF and the need to educate the Parents of Ahadhiyyah Students.

The main purpose to enrich and expand Islamic Knowledge and Practice within the Muslim population.


Your Contribution

Your contribution in this area is vital to achieve the desired objectives. Any contribution made towards enhancing Islamic Education will fall into the category of Sadakathul Jariya.

    Colombo District Ahadhiyyah Schools Federation

    CDASF is a District Organization overseeing the Administration of All Ahadhiyyah Schools in the District of Colombo. At present there are 32 Ahadhiyyah Schools in the District, more than the number of Government Muslims Schools in the District.

    Get Started

    Student Population

    The Colombo District has the largest number of Ahadhiyyah Schools with 32 in number and having a student population over 5000 students who study Islam

    Get Started

    Teacher Cadre

    Encouragingly most number of Ahadhiyyah Teachers are volunteers who spend their valuable knowledge time money and energy for the cause of Ahadhiyyah concept.

    There is an average of at least 10 Teachers in each Ahadhiyyah Schools and the total running up to nearly 350 Teachers

    Get Started

    Federation Management

    The management of each School is vested with the Principals of such schools but are monitored periodically. Monthly meetings are held to discuss various matters concerning the running of Ahadhiyyah Schools in each areas.

    Due to the absence of a permanent building the meetings were held in private residences of Principals Government schools and at present at a rented Room in Colombo 12. It is effectively administered with the help of various sub-committees represented from various Ahadhiyyah Schools whose representative are fully committed to the cause

    Federation Committee

    Welfare Committee

    This committee oversees the welfare of the Teachers by conducting programmes beneficial to the teachers such as Seminars, regular training / motivation / knowledge enhancing / charitable programmes

    Examination Committee

    Annual Examinations are held in every school and the examination papers are prepared by a selected group of Teachers and Principals

    Islamic Exhibition Committee

    An Islamic Exhibition comprising Arabic Calligraphy, Islamic Art & Architecture, Islamic History in order to exhibit the knowledge and creativity of the students. The exhibition will be held once in every Two years

    The Need for a Permanent Building

    Considering the multiplicity of activities of the CDASF, the need for a permanent building is felt. The proposed building will be used for the enhancement and development of Ahadhiyyah Education.

    There is a need for a Hall, for meetings and lectures, the need to establish a Teacher Training Institute, the need to conduct specialized Student development programmes, the need for a Permanent Office to administer the affairs of the CDASF and the need to educate the Parents of Ahadhiyyah Students.

    The main purpose to enrich and expand Islamic Knowledge and Practice within the Muslim population.

    Federation Activities

    Annual Ahadhiyyah Calendar

    During the last part of the year a Calendar for the following year is planned and prepared and given to all Ahadhiyyah Schools such that all schools will have an unformed activity

    Ahadhiyya Sports

    Since sports is one of the important component in human life, 25th December is declared as a Sports Day where all the students and the Teachers have the opportunity of getting together at a popular sport activity. The aim is to motivate the students and the Teachers

    State sponsored Teacher Training Programmes

    Recently the Western Provincial Council conducted a comprehensive Teacher Training Programme for nearly 300 Teachers selected from all Ahadhiyyah Schools in the Colombo District which enriched the knowledge and ability of the Teachers

    Regular Teacher Training Programmes

    The CDASF itself conducts several Teacher Training programmes sponsored by philanthropists which has improved the performance of the Teachers

    Students Activities

    Each Ahadhiyyah School conducts its own Annual Programmes such as Awards Day, Quiz Competition, Inter Ahadhiyyah Islamic Competitions, Islamic Art Exhibitions, Qirath and Quran recital competitions, etc....

    Ahadhiyyah Teacher Motivation (ATM) programme

    This is held annually as an In-House Training at an Environmentally Friendly location outside Colombo where apart from the academic guidance programmes, mind development, physical training programmes, motivational activities are organized successfully

    Newsletter

    A newsletter called "The Focus" is a regular Newsletter issued to disseminate information about the activities of the Ahadhiyyah Schools in the Colombo District to the general public
